Pangsua Pond

Looking for an escape from the stressful life?

Pangsua Pond is The Place to Go!


Originally built as a stormwater collection pond in the 1990s,
it is now the second-largest man-made floating wetland system with a 480m-long boardwalk

Here's a Sneak Peek of What It's Like in the Day




Things to Do When the Sun is Up:

  • Go for a Morning/Afternoon Run
  • Have a Picnic
  • Meditate

Alongside the sound of frogs croaking, crickets chirping, and the occasional buzzing of winged bloodsuckers,
the scene is rather calming.
The absence of whirring engines, curt honks of the impatient, the scent of burnt tyres against the asphalt road
will make you feel like you're in another realm,
away from the chaotic mundanities of everyday life.
Even the arrhythmic thumps against the wooden planks
as like-minded peace-seekers pass by added on to the serenity.
